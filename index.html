Avora Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Avora is a reminder and task management application. This policy explains what information the app handles, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

1. Information the app handles

Avora stores reminder titles, notes, task names, reminder dates and times, repeat settings, task state, app appearance settings, and app language settings on your device so the app can function.

If you use the optional "Call someone" reminder feature, Avora opens Android's system contact picker. Avora does not request broad contacts access. Avora only receives the contact name and phone number that you explicitly choose through the picker.

2. How information is used

Avora uses your reminder, task, and contact-selection data only to provide core app features such as:

  • showing reminders and tasks in the app
  • scheduling alerts and lock-screen alarm experiences
  • reopening the phone dialer for call reminders
  • restoring your information after device reboot
  • applying your selected theme and language preferences

3. Data sharing

Avora does not sell your personal information.

Avora does not share your reminders, tasks, or selected contact information with third parties from within the current app implementation.

4. Data storage and retention

Your reminder and task data is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage.

Data remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app.

5. Permissions and device capabilities

Avora may request or rely on the following Android permissions and capabilities:

  • Notifications, to deliver reminder alerts
  • Exact alarms, to trigger reminders at the scheduled time
  • Full-screen intent, to present alarm UI when appropriate
  • Wake lock and boot completed support, to reschedule and deliver alarms reliably after restart

Avora does not request READ_CONTACTS or CALL_PHONE. Contact selection uses the Android system picker, and calling uses the system dialer.

6. Your choices

  • delete reminders and tasks from within the app
  • disable or deny notification and alarm-related access in Android settings
  • clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored data

7. Children's privacy

Avora is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law in your region, and the app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect product, legal, or platform changes. The latest version should also be published on the public privacy policy URL used for app distribution.
